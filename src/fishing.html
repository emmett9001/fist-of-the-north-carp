Hokuto no Huchen (Fist of the North Carp)

Made @ Fishing Game Jam // Nina Freeman (me), Emmett Butler and audio by Deckman Coss

The inspiration for this game comes from a fishing trip I went on with my dad when I was little. He caught a sunfish and thought it would be funny to swing it around on the line. At the end of his swing the sunfish smacked me right on the cheek.

This game was made using Flixel, Adobe Photoshop and GIMP. You can view the Github repository and our Fishing Jam Forum post.
